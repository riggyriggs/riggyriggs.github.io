Problem

Usually when Freshmen go into school for the first time, some kids don’t have friends & that’s kinda a problem. No one wants to see a group of kids awkwardly staring at the wall not talking to anyone. It also affects their self-esteem, & makes them afraid of meeting people.

These kids don't even know where to start either! Freshmen's are usually shy when it comes to connecting...

Solution

Our app, Fember, can solve that problem. The app will help you connect with peers in the school. You and the random peer will be able to talk with one another anonymously and get to know each other. This app can get someone more friends during the beginning of the school or if they came to a new school.

They can talk about common interest and learn more about each other while talking. If the conversation goes well they’ll be prompted if they wanted to meet up in school and if each side answers yes it will reveal their real name and then they can meet at school the next day. If the convo didn’t go well they can just say no and forget about that person.
